5 best Tamil thriller movies on Netflix, ZEE5 & more with a nail-biting ending

Most nail-biting Tamil movies will keep you hooked

Priyadarshini Patwa
Published: Oct 21, 2021 | 12:52:06 IST

Tamil thriller movies are known for their unique plot and storytelling. With Tamil movies releasing pan-India, they have found a major following across regions. Over time, Kollywood has delivered several amazing Tamil thriller movies that have sent a shiver down our spine. Check out the list for the most nail-biting Tamil thriller movies on Netflix, ZEE5 and more.

5 best Tamil thriller movies on ZEE5, Netflix & more with a nail-biting ending

1. GAME OVER – NETFLIX


How often do we see a gamer forced to play a game and beat the antagonist to save her own life? Swapna, a game designer, suffers from PTSD and lives alone with her housemaid. She is on a turbulent road to discovery after suffering from an incident that changed her life. However, when a serial killer enters her house, she is forced to play a game and save her and her helper’s life. Directed by Ashwin Saravanan, the movie features Taapsee Pannu, Maala Parvathi, Sanchana Natarajan and Anish Kuruvilla.

2. 8 THOTAKKAL – DISNEY+ HOTSTAR

8 Thottakkal, translated to English as 8 bullets, is filled with thrilling moments that can give goosebumps to anyone. The movie is loosely inspired by the 1949 Japanese movie Stray Dog. Sathya, an honest policeman, accidentally loses his service pistol while tailing a criminal. The gun reaches Murthy, a killer who goes on a killing spree due to certain circumstances in his life.

3. MASTER – AMAZON PRIME VIDEO & ZEE5


While the movie received a mixed review, Master indeed makes for a good watch. Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi’s performance makes it worth it. JD, an alcoholic professor goes to a correctional facility to teach at a juvenile facility. He soon clashes with a ruthless gangster, who is using these children to expand his criminal empire.

4. U TURN – NETFLIX & AMAZON PRIME VIDEO

Starring Samantha Akkineni and directed by Pawan Kumar, this mystery thriller is a must-watch. When a flyover in the city becomes a hotspot for an accident, a reporter starts investigating the same. As she reaches close to the murder and mystery, she herself gets accused of killing a motorist on the flyover.

5. VIKRAM VEDHA – DISNEY+ HOTSTAR & ZEE5


Starring R. Madhavan, Vijay Sethupathi, Varalaxmi Sarathkumar and Shraddha Srinath, Vikram Vedha keeps you hooked till the end. Vikram, a special task force officer is on a hunt to capture Vedha. When Vedha surrenders himself, he tells him a story that engages Vikram in a mind game and challenges his notion of good and evil.
